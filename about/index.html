About Me

I am a computer engineering student at Texas A&M University. I have always wanted to become an engineer, but I was not always set on which type I wanted to be. In 4th grade I wanted to be a civil engineer. As I grew up, however, I witnessed the evolution of technology and its impact on my life. I eventually realized that it would be a shame if I did not learn about the principles that have shaped all aspects of my life.


I am particularly interested in controls and automation. I am fascinated on how electronics are capable of maintaining such precision even when challenged by chaotic conditions within the surrounding environment.
